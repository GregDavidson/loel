What is LOEL?

The League of Extraordinary Learners, LOEL, is a Learning Community of people who love to learn and who support one another in their learning activities.

The League consists of

  • Learners persuing their love of learning
  • Peer Facilitators assisting their peers with material they themselves have recently learned
  • Subject Experts & Learning Mentors
  • Supporters of LOEL and LOEL's Learners

Are you a Learner?

A Learner is someone who loves learning. Learners typically

  • Are fascinated by one or more subject areas
  • Love reading and exploring their areas of interest
  • Are eager to expand their Learning and Thinking skills

While the terms Learner and Student are partially synonymous, Student tends to imply someone following a specific progarm controlled by an Educational Institution. We use the term Learner to mean someone who is pursuing their interests without or beyond the scope of any official program.

Are you an Extraordinary Learner?

Extraordinary Learners possess

  • abundant curiosity & exuberance,
  • flexibility & resilience,
  • a drive to learn and grow.

While these characteristics are typical of young people, youthful older people often share the same characteristics.

The League is especially welcoming to young people and extends that welcome to youthful older people - as long as they are well behaved ☺.

How does the League support You and Other Extraordinary Learners?

Extraordinary Learners do not wait for teachers, they actively seek information and knowledge about their areas of interest. Modern Learners spend a lot of time browsing the Web, e.g. reading Wikipedia Articles, viewing TED talks, etc. Learners seek the best learning materials. Challenging material is sometimes learned best with the support of other Learners, Peer Facilitators, Experts and Mentors. Collaborative Projects can be helpful for some kinds of Learning.

Members of the League support one another in finding

  • The best Learning Materials for any Subject
  • Fellow Learners for Study Groups and collaborative Projects
  • Peer Facilitators who have recently learned the same Subjects
  • Expert Mentors who can back up the Peer Facilitators

We operate Strategically

We are careful to never stand between a Learner and their interests: the Learner is always the active agent in their education. We stand to the side and help make the resources which they need more available.

Much of the necessary resources already exist freely on the web but there are gaps. We will cause the creation or adaptation of

  • Directories of available material
  • Syllabi of organized courses of study
  • Materials for carrying out Hands-On Projects
  • Communication tools for
    • Participating in Group Projects
    • Interaction with Peer Facilitators and Mentors
  • Self-tests and other coverage metrics
  • Additional and improved material of all kinds

It is our thesis that the best peers and mentors for supporting the Learners are some of the other Learners who have recently completed a given course of study. We will call these graduates “Peer Facilitators”. We also believe that to the greatest extent possible, the Learners and the Peer Facilitators are the best creators of improved and missing materials, both during and soon after their experience with a course of study.

Learning in the Real World

Resources for a good education need to include more than excellent educational study materials. Much of the learning should be done as part of Hands-On Projects, often with Groups of Learners, dealing with the challenges of accomplishing results in the real world. Our partnership will empower and enable the Learners and Peer Facilitators to create and participate in a large variety of such projects.

We will encourage Learners in the same geographic area to create or join a LOEL Chapter and utilize local support to create and participate in

  • Weekend and Summer Intensives
  • Tech Shops, Maker Spaces, etc.

A LOEL Chapter will typically be small enough that its Learners can get to know one another. Some LOEL Chapters will be organized Geographically, e.g. San Diego LOEL or Mumbai LOEL; others will likely be Internet Based groups focused on common interests.

We also encourage and support Learners in acquiring the interpersonal communication and collaboration skills needed for success in carrying out large projects and in working effectively in large organizations such as Universities and Corporations.

How You Can Participate NOW!

Participation in LOEL can take many forms, including:

  • Using our Materials and Services
  • Improving LOEL's On-Line Learning Materials
  • Improving our On-Line Software Tools
  • Volunteering as a Peer Facilitator or a Subject Matter Expert
  • Participating at the Chapter Level or Creating New Chapters

We are initially using GitHub to host the LOEL. If you would like to participate, please send us a message or post an issue through this GitHub site.

We intend to move LOEL to The (Free and Open Source) Wicci System when that is finished. If you have an interest in the Wicci's technical areas, whether you're an expert or an eager learner, we can especially use your assistance!

We can also always use

  • Technical Writers and Artists
  • Expert Reviewers and Subject Domain Experts
  • Extraordinary Learners who would like to be Peer Facilitators
  • Fund Raisers, Enthusiastic Recruiters and more!
