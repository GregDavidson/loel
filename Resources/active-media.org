* Actively Engaging With Media

** Passive Consumption of Media is Soporific

When television first started becoming available to households, many
smart people expected a revolutionary leap forward in education and
enlightenment.  Television was going to make the wisdom of the
greatest of us available to everyone.  *Why did Television fail in its
promise?*

People who are not actively engaged with media they're "consuming" get
very little out of it.  Their minds enter a passive mode and only
shallowly process what they're receiving.  *No growth or learning takes place with engaged attention!*

*** Linear Media: Videos & Audio

Videos, Podcasts and Audio Books are Linear Media
- The material is presented in a fixed timeline
- They train the "consumer" to be passive
  - Thinking about something has you miss the next part!
