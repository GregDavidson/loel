* Your Role as a Peer Facilitator

A Peer Facilitator is normally someone who
- has recently completed a course or curriculum
- is willing to assist others for mutual benefit
- is excited by the material
- and wants /more/ understanding and mastery
      - more breadth, depth, scope
      - more ability to apply the material
      - consilience with other subjects
      - ease of using the material where it applies
      - increased ability to learn more!

A Peer Facilitator is
- A Learner on a Mastery Path
