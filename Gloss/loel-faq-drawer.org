* FAQ Drawers In LOEL Materials

- FAQ :: Frequently Asked Questions

LOEL Materials are centered around the needs of /Learners/.

When a Learner has a question about something in a /LOEL Document/ (Web Page,
OrgMode Document, etc.) they will look for a /FAQ Drawer/. A FAQ Drawer should
open when clicked on.

Opening a FAQ Drawer, Learners might find
- a relevant question is already in that FAQ Drawer!
- a useful answer to their question!
While the FAQ Drawer is open, Learners can
- Propose a new Question
- Propose a new Answer to any Question in the Drawer
- Rate or comment on existing Questions or Answers

The /Peer Facilitators/ and /Experts/ will use Learner submissions and feedback
to improve the usefulness of the content of the FAQ Drawer and the content which
it's attached to.
